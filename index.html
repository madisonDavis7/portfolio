My First App!

When I first began the process of creating an app I knew it needed to involve space in some way, I didn't have an exact idea in mind just that my love of astronomy would be the main theme. Eventually I landed on a sort of travel guide but in space, my team agreed it was good and we went from there. The name A Guide to the Galazy was decided on and the adventure truly began. The styling of the app was a natural process, I also love being creative so getting the desired design was simple, implementing it was harder but worth it. The app used Python, HTML/CSS and Django. It was a a process for sure but I learned so much. This app was a major accomplishment and made me realize I lean more towards frontend work than backend.

2024 Cybersec Challenge

In the spring of 2024 I did my first Cybersec Challenge at Missoula College with a few friends. I was very new to the world of linux, networking, and cybersecurity in general. It was more of a learning experience than anything. The goal was to find 'flags' in an ecrypted pi that we had to break into with no access to the internet. It was a challenging adventurea amd alot to take in but I had a fun time. Our team ended up placing 3rd and I hope to continue to attend these challenges.
