About the Project

Pulse is a music portfolio I created to work on my skills. It's a personal project meant to demonstrate my ability to create visually appealing portfolio for potential clients. It took a few weeks to complete and went through many design iterations as I found what did and did not work.

This is a pretty basic website made with HTML, CSS, and minimal JavaScript. I wanted to kep the tech stack simple and focus more on the design process and not the technical difficulty itself. My main goal for this was to really put thought into the colors, typography, visuals, and overall user experience.

Pulse

When I started the creative process I created a mood board to help me visualize the direction I wanted to go. My aim for this project was to dedicate time to the design and feel of the site itself. I focused the creative process, user research, and having a fully thought out plan before I began coding.

Wireframe

The wireframe for this idea went through many changes as I tried out different layouts. I wanted it to be fairly minimal and modern, but also have sufficient text space. The whole design process was done in Figma, from my inspiration page to the various wireframes for different screen sizes. I needed to make sure that for every device and possible display size, the layout was clean and responsive.

When I began this website my first step was a mood board to get a feel for the tone I wanted to set for users. I spent many hours on Pinterest and searching through various photography sites to find images, color palettes, and typography that fit what I had in mind. I knew I wanted it to be a darker background with some bright colors that popped and contrasted well. The portfolio was designed for an edm artist and I needed that to be reflected in the design. The first step is to get an idea, I through everything onto one page and use elements from different images to create a cohesive design.
